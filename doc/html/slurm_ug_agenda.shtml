Slurm User Group Meeting 2020

The Slurm User Group Meeting (SLUG'20) this fall will be moving online, and will not be hosted in-person at Harvard as scheduled, due to uncertainty around scheduling events caused by COVID-19.

In lieu of an in-person meeting, SchedMD will broadcast a select set of presentations on Tuesday, September 15th, 2020 through YouTube Live from the SchedMD Slurm channel. No registration is required.

Viewers are encouraged to ask questions through the live chat feature. SchedMD staff will be moderating the chat, and will forward relevant and timely questions to the presenter. The last 5-10 minutes of each presentation will be reserved for further Q+A, as well as provide a brief break between each session. (Unfortunately, we cannot provide snacks in the hallway for virtual SLUG; you will need to raid your own pantry instead.)

Please note that each presentation is contained in a separate broadcast, and you will need to switch between them at the breaks.

These presentations will remain online for one week after the live broadcast.
